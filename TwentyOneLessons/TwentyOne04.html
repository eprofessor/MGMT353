Exercise 1-Chapters 15 to 18

We will discuss this material during class.

Technological advances are changing our world at a rate never before experienced.

The twin revolutions in information technology and biotechnology confront us with the biggest challenges our species has ever encountered.
People feel overwhelmed and confused by the current global predicament.
Global processes are too complicated for any single person to understand.
How can you know the truth about the world, and avoid falling victim to propaganda and misinformation?

15. (50) Ignorance

Individual humans know little about the world, and fail to realize how ignorant they are of the current situation.
Most views are formed by groupthink rather than rationality, even among scientists.
Political power distorts the truth as people try to advance their own interests.


What problem does ignorance cause for leaders?

16. (50) Justice

In previous time there was a more direct link between personal actions and a sense of justice.
Today's global interconnected world is far too complicated for our hunter-gatherer brains.
Large-scale structural biases, which are difficult to detect, cause most injustices.


Is it possible to understand the truth and find justice in today's world?

17. (50) Post-Truth

Bald lies have been used to serve a "higher" truth.
One such lie was that Russian troops masquerading as Ukranian "self-defense" groups" was justified on the basis that it would preserve the sacred Russian nation.
Ukranians and others strongly disagree, but who is correct?
Propaganda and disinformation are not recent developments.


Why do scientists need to be more involved in current public debates?

18. (50) Science Fiction

Science fiction movies do more to shape how people understand the most important technological developments of our time.
The human mind has been shaped by history and biology.
Media such as 'Inside Out', 'Brave New World' and the 'Matrix' make us confront notions of free will versus controlled environments. Which is better?


What is the ultimate human prison?
